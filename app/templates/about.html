About OKOnsen

♨️ Our Mission

OKOnsen is a passion project born from a deep appreciation for Japanese hot springs and traditional ryokans. Our mission is to help travelers discover the perfect onsen—whether you're looking for a private bath, a tattoo-friendly spot, or a stunning view of Mount Fuji.

🌟 What We Do

We curate and map the best hot springs across Japan. For each location, we provide practical information and unique insights to make your stay more relaxing and memorable.
